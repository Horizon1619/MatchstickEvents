# Purpose

This document defines the visual and emotional identity of the MatchStick Events brand.

It establishes how the company should be perceived across the website and ensures a consistent experience regardless of who designs or develops future pages.

This document focuses on **brand identity**, not interface implementation.

---

# Brand Foundation

## Brand Name

MatchStick Events

---

## Tagline

> **Moments. Memories. Magic.**

This tagline should remain consistent across all branding materials. 0

---

## Mission

Transform beautiful ideas into unforgettable celebrations that inspire awe and become lifelong memories.

This mission is adapted from the client brief while preserving its intent. 1

---

# Brand Positioning

MatchStick Events is positioned as a **premium event planning company** that delivers bespoke celebrations rather than standardized event packages.

The company should be perceived as:

- Creative
- Elegant
- Sophisticated
- Detail-oriented
- Trustworthy
- Luxury-focused

---

# Brand Personality

Imagine MatchStick Events as a person.

It would be:

- Calm
- Confident
- Refined
- Artistic
- Welcoming
- Experienced
- Passionate
- Professional

It should never feel:

- Loud
- Cheap
- Overly playful
- Flashy
- Chaotic
- Sales-driven

---

# Brand Promise

Every interaction should reinforce one simple promise:

> "Your celebration deserves to become an unforgettable memory."

---

# Emotional Goals

The brand should consistently evoke:

- Wonder
- Joy
- Trust
- Warmth
- Celebration
- Elegance
- Confidence

Visitors should leave with the feeling that their event will receive exceptional care and creativity.

---

# Brand Voice

The writing style should be:

- Warm
- Human
- Confident
- Inspirational
- Refined
- Conversational

Avoid:

- Corporate jargon
- Aggressive sales language
- Buzzwords
- Overly technical explanations

---

# Tone of Voice

Depending on context:

## Homepage

Inspirational.

## About

Authentic.

## Services

Helpful.

## Gallery

Minimal.

## Consultation

Encouraging.

## Contact

Friendly.

---

# Visual Identity

The client requests branding that feels elegant, modern and dream-like while avoiding messy layouts or colours that feel out of place. 2

The visual language should therefore prioritize:

- Clean layouts
- Large imagery
- Spacious compositions
- Premium typography
- Sophisticated colour usage
- Subtle motion

---

# Logo Usage

The official client logo should always be used without distortion.

Guidelines:

- Maintain adequate clear space.
- Preserve aspect ratio.
- Do not stretch.
- Do not rotate.
- Do not recolour without approval.
- Do not place over visually busy backgrounds.

---

# Photography Style

Photography should communicate:

- Emotion
- Luxury
- Detail
- Celebration
- Human connection

Preferred characteristics:

- Natural lighting
- Rich textures
- Elegant décor
- Genuine moments
- High resolution

Avoid:

- Low-quality images
- Heavy filters
- Stock-looking photographs
- Cluttered compositions

---

# Colour Philosophy

The colour palette should communicate:

- Luxury
- Calmness
- Sophistication
- Warmth

Primary colours should remain neutral and timeless.

Accent colours should be used sparingly to guide attention rather than dominate the interface.

The exact colour system will be defined in **06-design-system.md**.

---

# Typography Philosophy

Typography should communicate:

- Elegance
- Readability
- Premium quality

Headings should feel refined.

Body text should remain effortless to read.

Specific font families, sizing and spacing are documented separately in **06-design-system.md**.

---

# Iconography

Icons should be:

- Minimal
- Elegant
- Consistent
- Easy to recognise

Avoid decorative or cartoon-style icon sets.

---

# Illustration Style

If illustrations are used, they should be:

- Minimal
- Sophisticated
- Soft
- Monoline
- Secondary to photography

Photography should remain the dominant visual medium.

---

# Brand Consistency Rules

Every future page should satisfy the following:

- Consistent logo usage.
- Consistent writing style.
- Consistent imagery.
- Consistent emotional tone.
- Consistent navigation language.
- Consistent premium presentation.

---

# Brand Do's

- Tell stories.
- Showcase craftsmanship.
- Use authentic imagery.
- Inspire confidence.
- Celebrate meaningful moments.
- Keep messaging concise.

---

# Brand Don'ts

- Compete on price.
- Use excessive marketing language.
- Overload pages with text.
- Sacrifice elegance for visual effects.
- Make unrealistic promises.

---

# Accessibility Considerations

The brand should remain premium while also being accessible.

This includes:

- Readable typography.
- Sufficient colour contrast.
- Clear language.
- Inclusive imagery.
- Responsive presentation.

---

# Future Brand Evolution

The brand identity should be flexible enough to support future additions, including:

- Blog
- Awards
- Testimonials
- Press coverage
- Careers
- Luxury destination events

without requiring a complete redesign.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

This document is complete when:

- Brand personality is defined.
- Voice and tone are documented.
- Photography style is documented.
- Logo usage guidelines are established.
- Visual philosophy is documented.
- Brand consistency rules are defined.
