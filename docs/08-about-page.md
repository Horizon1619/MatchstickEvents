# Purpose

The About page exists to transform curiosity into trust.

Unlike the Homepage, which creates an emotional first impression, the About page should answer the visitor's next question:

> "Who are these people, and why should I trust them with one of the most important days of my life?"

This page should tell a story rather than present facts.

---

# Business Goals

The About page should:

- Build credibility.
- Humanize the company.
- Introduce the founder.
- Explain the company's philosophy.
- Strengthen emotional connection.
- Increase consultation confidence.

---

# User Goals

After reading this page, visitors should understand:

- Who MatchStick Events is.
- Why the company exists.
- What makes it different.
- The philosophy behind every celebration.
- Why they should begin a consultation.

# Hero Section

## Purpose

Immediately establish the personality of the company.

---

## Background

Large cinematic image.

Preferably one that captures:

- Emotion
- Celebration
- Elegant décor
- Human connection

Avoid text-heavy hero banners.

---

## Hero Heading

Example direction

```
Every Celebration Has A Story.
We Simply Help Tell It Beautifully.
```

This is design guidance only.

---

## Supporting Text

One short paragraph introducing MatchStick Events.

The company was founded in 2012 by **Preksha Chhajer**, with a mission centered on turning beautiful ideas into unforgettable events. 1

---

# Company Story

## Purpose

Explain why MatchStick Events exists.

The source document asks for the company description to be expanded. This section should therefore build a compelling narrative while remaining faithful to the company's mission. 2

---

## Storytelling Guidelines

Do not present a timeline.

Instead, tell a story.

The writing should feel:

- Warm
- Honest
- Elegant
- Personal

Avoid:

- Corporate language
- Marketing clichés
- Exaggerated claims

---

## Suggested Topics

- Love for meaningful celebrations.
- Attention to details.
- Creating experiences rather than decorations.
- Transforming dreams into reality.
- Building lifelong memories.

These themes are derived from the company's mission and brand direction. 3
