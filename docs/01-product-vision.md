# Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the long-term vision of the MatchStick Events website.

Rather than describing technical implementation, this document explains **why the website exists**, **what it should achieve**, and **how users should feel** when interacting with it.

Every future design and development decision should support the vision described here.

---

# Company Overview

MatchStick Events is a premium event planning company headquartered in Kolkata, founded in 2012 by **Preksha Chhajer**. The company's stated mission is to transform ideas into memorable event experiences, and its official tagline is:

> **Moments. Memories. Magic.**

The client brief positions the company as an event planner with an emphasis on elegant, dream-like experiences and encourages expanding the brand story while maintaining a modern, refined identity. 0

---

# Vision Statement

To create a digital experience that reflects the same elegance, creativity and craftsmanship that MatchStick Events delivers in the real world.

Visitors should leave the website feeling inspired, emotionally connected and confident that MatchStick Events can transform their celebration into an unforgettable experience.

---

# Product Mission

The website should not function as an online catalogue.

Instead, it should become the digital flagship of the brand.

Its primary responsibility is to inspire visitors, establish credibility and encourage them to begin a consultation.

---

# Core Product Philosophy

The website should communicate emotion before information.

Visitors should experience the feeling of luxury before reading detailed descriptions.

The visual language, photography, typography, spacing and animations should work together to create an atmosphere that feels calm, sophisticated and memorable.

---

# Business Vision

The website should become one of the company's strongest business assets.

It should:

- Strengthen the MatchStick Events brand.

- Showcase the quality of previous work.

- Increase qualified consultation enquiries.

- Improve customer trust.

- Reduce dependence on social media as the primary business channel.

- Support long-term business growth.

Current enquiries mainly arrive through word of mouth, Instagram, calls, WhatsApp and email. The website should complement and strengthen these channels rather than replace them. 1

---

# Experience Vision

The website should feel like entering a beautifully designed event.

Every section should create curiosity.

Every interaction should feel intentional.

Every animation should support storytelling.

Nothing should feel rushed or overwhelming.

The visitor should naturally progress from admiration to trust and finally to requesting a consultation.

---

# Emotional Goals

Visitors should experience:

- Wonder

- Excitement

- Trust

- Calmness

- Luxury

- Personal connection

- Confidence

The website should never feel:

- Cheap

- Busy

- Generic

- Corporate

- Overly playful

- Visually noisy

---

# Brand Personality

The digital personality of MatchStick Events should be:

- Elegant

- Modern

- Artistic

- Sophisticated

- Warm

- Approachable

- Detail-oriented

- Premium

These characteristics are derived from the client's branding direction, which asks for something elegant, modern and dream-like without becoming visually messy. 2

---

# Target Audience

The primary audience includes:

- Couples planning weddings.

- Families planning milestone celebrations.

- Parents organising birthdays or baby showers.

- Corporate organisations.

- Luxury private clients.

- Individuals seeking bespoke event experiences.

The website should appeal to clients who value quality, aesthetics and personalised service over low-cost event packages.

---

# Product Positioning

The MatchStick Events website should position the company as a premium creative studio for celebrations rather than a conventional event management company.

The emphasis should be on craftsmanship, creativity and personalised experiences.

---

# Success Criteria

The website will be considered successful if it:

- Creates an exceptional first impression.

- Encourages visitors to explore multiple pages.

- Increases consultation enquiries.

- Demonstrates the quality of previous work.

- Builds trust before the first conversation.

- Reinforces the luxury identity of the brand.

---

# Out of Scope

The following are intentionally outside the scope of this website:

- Public pricing.

- Online event package purchasing.

- Instant event booking.

- Customer self-service portal.

- E-commerce functionality.

The client brief specifies a consultation-focused workflow rather than public pricing or direct online booking. 3

---

# Guiding Principles

Every future design and development decision should satisfy the following principles:

1. Simplicity over complexity.

2. Quality over quantity.

3. Emotion over information.

4. Storytelling over advertising.

5. Consistency over novelty.

6. Accessibility without sacrificing elegance.

7. Mobile-first design.

8. Fast, responsive interactions.

---

# Design Decisions

The following are design decisions introduced by this documentation and are **not direct client requirements**:

- Mobile-first implementation.

- Consultation-first customer journey.

- Cinematic scrolling experience.

- Premium micro-interactions.

- Modern component-based architecture.

- AI-assisted "Dream Planner" feature (to be specified separately).

These decisions are intended to strengthen the product while remaining aligned with the client's goals.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

This document is considered fulfilled when:

- Every future feature aligns with the product vision.

- Every page reinforces the premium brand identity.

- No feature contradicts the core philosophy.

- Business objectives remain the primary decision driver.

- The website consistently reflects the values defined in this document.
