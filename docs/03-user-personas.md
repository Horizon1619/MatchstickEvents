# Purpose

This document defines the primary users of the MatchStick Events website.

The goal is to understand who visits the website, why they visit, what they expect, and what problems they are trying to solve.

These personas guide every future UX, UI, content, and feature decision.

---

# Overview

The client provides luxury event planning services for weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, baby showers, corporate events, high teas, and festive celebrations. Therefore, the website must support multiple visitor types while maintaining one consistent premium experience. 0

---

# Persona 1 — Wedding Client

## Overview

A couple planning one of the most important events of their lives.

Usually researching multiple planners before making contact.

---

## Goals

- Find a trustworthy planner.
- View previous wedding work.
- Understand the company's style.
- Feel emotionally connected.
- Schedule a consultation.

---

## Pain Points

- Too many generic event planners.
- Difficulty judging quality online.
- Fear of poor execution.
- Limited time.

---

## Website Expectations

- Beautiful photography.
- Premium visual design.
- Clear process.
- Luxury branding.
- Easy consultation request.

---

# Persona 2 — Family Celebration Planner

## Overview

A family member organising:

- Anniversary
- Birthday
- Baby Shower
- Milestone Celebration

---

## Goals

- Create a memorable celebration.
- Reduce planning stress.
- Find creative ideas.
- Work with experienced professionals.

---

## Pain Points

- Limited planning experience.
- Time constraints.
- Finding reliable vendors.
- Coordinating family expectations.

---

## Website Expectations

- Inspiration.
- Gallery.
- Previous events.
- Simple enquiry process.

---

# Persona 3 — Corporate Client

## Overview

An HR Manager, Marketing Manager, Founder or Executive planning:

- Product Launch
- Corporate Gala
- Awards Ceremony
- Office Celebration

The client brief includes corporate events among the core services. 1

---

## Goals

- Professional execution.
- Reliable timeline.
- Strong branding.
- Smooth communication.

---

## Pain Points

- Strict deadlines.
- Budget accountability.
- Reputation risk.
- Large guest counts.

---

## Website Expectations

- Professional presentation.
- Portfolio credibility.
- Clear contact information.
- Efficient consultation process.

---

# Persona 4 — Luxury Lifestyle Client

## Overview

An individual seeking a bespoke event experience.

This visitor values originality, aesthetics and exclusivity over price.

---

## Goals

- Create a unique celebration.
- Impress guests.
- Receive personalised service.
- Work with creative professionals.

---

## Pain Points

- Generic event concepts.
- Poor attention to detail.
- Lack of creativity.

---

## Website Expectations

- Premium branding.
- Exceptional imagery.
- Elegant presentation.
- High-end storytelling.

---

# Persona 5 — Returning Client

## Overview

A previous MatchStick Events customer planning another event.

---

## Goals

- Reconnect quickly.
- Explore new ideas.
- Contact the team.

---

## Pain Points

- Repeating information.
- Finding updated contact details.

---

## Website Expectations

- Familiar branding.
- Quick access to consultation.
- Updated portfolio.

---

# Persona 6 — Curious Visitor

## Overview

Someone who discovers MatchStick Events through Instagram, Facebook, Google or referrals.

The client currently receives enquiries through social media, calls, WhatsApp, email and word of mouth. 2 3

---

## Goals

- Learn about the company.
- Browse previous work.
- Decide whether to enquire.

---

## Pain Points

- Uncertainty about quality.
- Lack of information.

---

## Website Expectations

- Fast loading.
- Mobile-friendly experience.
- Easy navigation.
- Beautiful visuals.

---

# Shared User Goals

Regardless of persona, almost every visitor wants to:

- Trust the company.
- View previous work.
- Understand available services.
- Feel inspired.
- Contact the business easily.

---

# Shared User Frustrations

Visitors generally dislike:

- Slow websites.
- Confusing navigation.
- Poor-quality photographs.
- Outdated information.
- Generic designs.
- Too much text.
- Hidden contact details.

---

# User Motivations

Visitors are motivated by:

- Creating unforgettable memories.
- Reducing planning stress.
- Working with experienced professionals.
- Achieving beautiful event outcomes.
- Receiving personalised attention.

---

# Accessibility Considerations

The website should accommodate users with different devices and abilities by providing:

- Mobile-first layouts.
- Readable typography.
- Accessible colour contrast.
- Keyboard-friendly navigation.
- Descriptive image text.
- Fast loading performance.

| Priority | Persona |

| High | Corporate Client |
| Medium | Luxury Lifestyle Client |
| Medium | Returning Client |
| Low | Curious Visitor |

---

# Design Implications

These personas imply that the website should:

- Build trust quickly.
- Showcase visual quality before detailed information.
- Keep consultation accessible from every page.
- Emphasise completed work.
- Reduce the effort required to make an enquiry.
